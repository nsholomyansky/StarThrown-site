Cast to the Stars. Conquer what is yours.

Competitive Mobile Strategy Game

Humanity was cast out into the stars by AI and left to survive in stardoms scattered across the void. Now the fight begins to take back Earth. Strengthen your stardom, forge alliances with real players, and push against the machines and rival alliances.

Become the strongest Stardom by growing your fleet, technology, and resources, and earning score through battle. Be part of the most fearsome alliance — attack together, defend each other, capture territory, improve alliance technology, and be the highest scoring alliance.

Star Thrown is a round-based game, giving players a set amount of time to conquer the galaxy. At the end of the round, the most dominant Stardom and the best alliance are the winners. Then do it all again — every new round brings a fresh start, a chance to try a new strategy, and changes that may include new ship types, rebalanced stats, and new features.

And unlike most strategy games, we strive to make the playing field fair. Your stardom has real value no matter what anyone else spends.
