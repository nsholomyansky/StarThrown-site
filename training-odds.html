How Captain Training Works

Each Training Credit you spend initiates one training pull. The outcome is selected randomly on our servers using the probability weights in the tables below. Every pull yields exactly one outcome — either a quantity of Captain Experience or the unlock of a specific captain.

Training Credits can be obtained through normal gameplay and can also be purchased with real money in the in-game shop. The odds below are identical regardless of how your credits were obtained.

Already own that captain? A pull that lands on a captain you have not yet unlocked grants that captain plus the Experience on Unlock shown in the table below. If you already own the captain, you receive 1,000 Captain Experience instead — the unlock bonus does not apply to duplicate pulls.
